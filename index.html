Our Story

An ancient idea.
A modern practice.

The Telesterion was a great hall near ancient Athens where something remarkable happened for more than 2,000 years. People of every walk of life — young and old, slaves and Roman emperors — would enter, and leave changed. They returned to all corners of the world carrying new knowledge and a fearlessness that informed everyone around them.

That journey was an inflection point — and those who made it were never the same. That is what good strategy does. Telesterion Advisory exists to create those moments for organizations of every kind: startups building from scratch, and going concerns ready for a turnaround. As a business generalist, Joe works across industries and challenges, with particular depth in strategy, communications, and partnerships — the three levers that most reliably free owners and directors to do what they do best.

What We Do

Services
built for growth

As a business generalist, Telesterion Advisory works with startups and going concerns alike. Whether you need a strategy built from the ground up or a turnaround that gets your organization moving again, our work centers on three core strengths: strategy, communications, and partnerships.

01
Strategic Planning

02
Stakeholder Engagement & Partnership Building
Identifying, cultivating, and structuring the relationships that move organizations forward — from community partners to institutional allies and funders.
03
Communications & Advertising
Crafting the messages, channels, and campaigns that build trust, grow audiences, and position your organization with clarity and conviction.
04
Turnarounds
For going concerns that have lost momentum — we diagnose what's holding the organization back and build a clear path to renewed health and performance.
05
Operational Transformation
Process improvement, organisational design, and systems implementation to build a business that scales efficiently.
06
Executive Coaching
One-to-one advisory for founders and senior leaders navigating complex decisions, team challenges, and growth inflections.
